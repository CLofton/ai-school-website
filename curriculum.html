Curriculum

A practical AI adoption curriculum for modern teams.

Austin AI School programs are modular. Start with literacy, deepen with workflow labs, then build technical systems when the use case justifies it.

Build a custom cohort Compare tracks

Tracks

Choose the depth your team needs.

Each track can run as a standalone workshop or as part of a longer enterprise enablement roadmap.

1 dayLeaders

AI Literacy and Strategy

For executives, managers, and cross-functional leaders who need a clear, practical model for AI adoption.

  • AI capabilities and limits
  • Risk, data, and governance basics
  • Use-case prioritization matrix
  • 90-day adoption plan
3 daysDepartments

Applied Workflow Labs

For teams that need to apply AI to recurring work across finance, HR, sales, operations, and administration.

  • Workflow mapping and redesign
  • Prompt systems and QA loops
  • Reusable department playbooks
  • Measurement and adoption rituals
5 daysBuilders

AI Agent Engineering

For technical teams ready to prototype and evaluate AI systems that retrieve data, use tools, and complete tasks.

  • LLM APIs and structured outputs
  • Embeddings, retrieval, and RAG
  • Agent planning and tool use
  • Evaluations, monitoring, and guardrails

Enterprise Sequence
