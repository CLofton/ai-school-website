The Role

What is a Forward Deployed Engineer?

An FDE lives at the intersection of engineering, consulting, and product. They embed with a client, understand their business, and build custom AI systems that actually ship.

"While Devs focus on creating a single capability for many customers, FDEs focus on enabling many capabilities for a single customer."

Forward Deployed Engineer

Deploys custom AI systems on-site. Owns integration, scoping, and stakeholder communication. Ships production agents.

FDE

Software Engineer

Builds product features for many users. Deep on code, less on client engagement. Ships general-purpose software.

SWE

Solutions Engineer

Pre-sales technical support. Demonstrates product capability. Configures, but rarely builds custom systems.
