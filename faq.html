Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Austin AI School, our programs, and how to get started.

AI training at Austin AI School covers the practical skills needed to understand, use, and build AI systems. Depending on the program, you'll learn AI literacy (understanding what AI can and can't do), how to use AI tools effectively in your daily work, and — for technical tracks — how to build AI agents, work with LLM APIs, create RAG systems, and deploy production AI applications. Our focus is always on hands-on, practical skills you can apply immediately.
Our programs serve a wide range of learners. Corporate training is designed for business teams — executives, managers, and engineers who need to upskill in AI. Workforce development programs serve career changers, job seekers, veterans, returning citizens, and anyone looking to build AI skills for the modern economy. Our weekly webinars are open to everyone, regardless of experience level.
Not for all programs. Our AI Literacy Workshop and many workforce development programs require zero coding experience — they focus on understanding AI concepts and using AI tools effectively. Our more technical programs (AI Implementation and AI Engineering Intensive) do involve some coding, primarily in Python. We'll let you know the prerequisites for each program upfront so you can choose the right fit.
Corporate training pricing depends on the program: the 1-day AI Literacy Workshop is $500/person, the 3-day AI Implementation Program is $2,000/person, and the 5-day AI Engineering Intensive is $5,000/person. We also offer custom enterprise programs with volume pricing for larger teams. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your organization's needs.
