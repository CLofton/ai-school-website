💰 Financing

We offer multiple ways to make AI School affordable:

  • Income Share Agreement (ISA): Pay nothing upfront. Pay 15% of your salary for 24 months after you land a job paying $50K+.
  • Monthly Payment Plan: Split tuition into 6 or 12 monthly installments with 0% interest.
  • Employer Sponsorship: We provide a formal proposal template for your employer. Many companies cover 50-100% of tuition.
  • Scholarships: $2,000-$5,000 scholarships available for veterans, underrepresented groups, and career changers from non-tech backgrounds.
  • Early Bird Discount: $1,500 off when you apply 8+ weeks before cohort start.
