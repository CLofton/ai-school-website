About Austin AI School

Austin AI School is a workforce development and corporate training organization dedicated to making AI skills accessible to everyone — from Fortune 500 engineering teams to career changers to returning citizens.

Founded by Chris Lofton, who has managed education programs across 240+ locations and trained 10,000+ students, Austin AI School brings institutional-grade training to the AI revolution.

Every Tuesday at 7pm, we host free community webinars. Every week, we train teams. Every month, new AI engineers enter the workforce. That's our mission — and we're just getting started.
