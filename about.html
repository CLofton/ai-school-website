Our Mission

Democratizing AI Engineering

Austin AI School was founded with a simple belief: the AI revolution shouldn't leave anyone behind. While Fortune 500 companies race to deploy AI agent systems, the talent pipeline can't keep up. We're here to change that.

Our programs are designed to take motivated individuals — regardless of their background — and transform them into production-ready AI engineers in weeks, not years. We don't teach abstract theory. We teach you to build the autonomous systems that are reshaping every industry.

Based in Austin, Texas — one of the fastest-growing tech hubs in the world — we sit at the intersection of innovation, opportunity, and community.
