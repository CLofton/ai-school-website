About Austin AI School

Making AI skills accessible to everyone — from Fortune 500 teams to career changers to underserved communities.

Our Mission

AI Training for Everyone

The AI revolution is here — but most people are being left behind. Enterprise teams need to upskill fast. Workers need new career paths. Communities need access to the skills that will define the next decade of the economy.

Austin AI School exists to close that gap. We deliver practical, hands-on AI training to corporations, workforce development organizations, and individuals. No fluff. No hype. Just the skills you need to work with AI — starting today.

Based in Austin, TX — the tech capital of the South — we're building the training infrastructure for the AI-powered workforce.

Meet Chris Lofton

Chris Lofton

Founder & CEO

Chris Lofton has spent his career at the intersection of education and technology. As a senior education leader, he managed training programs across 240+ locations nationwide, building systems that delivered consistent, high-quality education at scale.

Over his career, Chris has been responsible for training 10,000+ students — from corporate professionals to career changers to underserved populations. He knows what it takes to build training programs that actually work: clear outcomes, hands-on practice, and accountability.

Now, Chris is applying that operational expertise to the biggest skills challenge of our generation: artificial intelligence. Austin AI School combines institutional-grade training infrastructure with cutting-edge AI curriculum to prepare the modern workforce for an AI-powered future.

His mission is simple: make AI skills accessible to everyone — regardless of background, experience level, or zip code.
